Objective

Keep the opus core intact as the magnum mechanism accelerates, avoid incoming pistons, saw-arms, and molten drops, collect risky bonus notes for combo, and post the highest survival score on a simple local leaderboard with reactive chime-cy

Controls

  • Shift lanes: Swipe left or right -> The opus core snaps to the next rail to dodge hazards or line up note pickups.
  • Risk charge: Swipe upward -> Move into the inner magnum rail, where hazards are denser but score multiplier builds faster.
  • Safety drop: Swipe downward -> Move back to the outer rail, lowering risk but slowing multiplier gain.

How to Play

  1. Start on the middle rail of a giant magnum machine with three circular running lanes.
  2. Hazards stream inward in repeating mechanical patterns: piston stamps, rotating cutter heads, chain hooks, and ember bursts.
  3. Swipe left or right to move around each lane and avoid contact; one hit cracks the opus core, a second hit ends the run.
  4. Blue opus notes appear near hazard edges. Collecting them without getting hit increases combo and restores a small amount of crack meter.
  5. The inner magnum rail grants a rising score multiplier while occupied, but hazard density and speed are highest there.
  6. Every 30 seconds the mechanism adds a new hazard type and the soundtrack layers in sharper metallic percussion to signal escalation.
  7. The run ends when the core takes its final break or when the 4-minute magnum cycle completes; score is saved to a fast local leaderboard.

Tips

  • Stay on the outer rail early, then dive inward during readable patterns to bank multiplier.
  • Blue notes that sit beside pistons are the safest way to keep combo alive.
  • Audio matters: heavy bass clunks warn piston drops, while high chimes mark note spawns.
  • If the core is cracked, prioritize survival until a safe note cluster appears.
