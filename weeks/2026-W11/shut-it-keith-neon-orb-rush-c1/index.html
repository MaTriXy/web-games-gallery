    Mission briefing

    Ready to launch?

    Survive a 3-minute set by silencing Keith's neon chatter: tap every word-orb he spits out before it vanishes or reaches the SHUT bar, build combos, and chase a leaderboard score.

    Controls

    • Pop a word-orb: Tap a moving neon orb -> The orb bursts, the SHUT bar cools, and your combo increases.
    • Silence a rush lane: Double-tap a flashing red orb -> Stops a dangerous fast orb worth bonus points.
    • Use mute pulse: Tap and hold the SHUT button when charged -> Briefly freezes all active orbs for cleanup.

    Plan

    1. Keith stands center stage and telegraphs each spit pattern with a mouth glow and lane highlight 0.5 seconds before launch.
    2. Neon word-orbs arc toward the SHUT bar at the bottom; tap them before they fade out or cross the bar.
    3. Every clean hit adds score and combo; every 10-hit combo raises the multiplier by 1x up to 5x.

    Pilot tips

    • Watch Keith's mouth color: blue means wide spread, orange means straight rush, red means a fast danger orb.
    • Protect your combo first; missing is worse than skipping a low-value edge orb.
    • Save the mute pulse for overlapping red rush waves after the 90-second mark.
