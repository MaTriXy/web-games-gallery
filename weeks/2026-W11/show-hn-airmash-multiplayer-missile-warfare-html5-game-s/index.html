Objective

In 2-4 minute rounds, scan a crowded top-down dogfight and tap only friendly aircraft broadcasting your team's relay signal while avoiding enemy decoys, jammed planes, and live missiles.

Controls

  • Tag relay target: Tap a blue aircraft with a pulsing ring -> Scores points, builds combo, and refreshes the signal timer
  • Ignore false signal: Do not tap red decoy planes, gray jammed planes, or missiles -> Keeps lives intact and preserves multiplier
  • Panic clear: Two-finger tap when the meter is full -> Briefly slows the airspace and reveals valid targets

How to Play

  1. Friendly multiplayer pilots enter from all sides, weaving through an active airmash battle.
  2. Only aircraft showing the current relay code pulse are valid targets; tap them before the pulse expires.
  3. Each correct tag extends your combo and speeds up incoming traffic.
  4. Enemy jets begin spoofing signals, missiles cross lanes, and weather static hides pulses.
  5. A wrong tap or a missed valid pilot costs a life; lose all lives and the round ends.
  6. Submit your score to the lightweight leaderboard and restart instantly offline or online.

Tips

  • Watch for ring color first, plane shape second; enemies mimic silhouettes before they mimic timing.
  • Save Panic Clear for missile-heavy waves when valid targets overlap.
  • Short, accurate streaks score better than frantic tapping in late waves.
