    Controls

    • Lane dodge: Swipe left or right -> Dash to the next sky lane to avoid incoming hazards.
    • Brake hop: Swipe down -> Drop to the lower catwalk for a brief dodge window under high hazards.
    • Seal grab: Swipe into a glowing seal token -> Collect an Official Seal to raise the multiplier and refill a small combo timer.

    Plan

    1. The run starts on the Highguard rooftop as sirens pulse and slow drums mark the official shutdown countdown.
    2. Hazards stream in from above and ahead: broken drones, collapsing signs, blackout beams, and falling antenna chunks.
    3. Every 20 seconds the shutdown level rises, increasing hazard speed, mixing lane patterns, and adding tighter beam sequences.

    Pilot tips

    • Swipe early; late lane changes get trapped by beam pairs.
    • Use the lower catwalk only to pass under high hazards, then return to center where reads are cleaner.
    • Chase Official Seals when the path after them is open; a broken combo costs more than one missed pickup.
