    Controls

    • Dodge: Swipe left or right -> The heartbeat snaps to a neighboring body lane to avoid incoming hazards.
    • Dive: Swipe up or down at junctions -> The heartbeat shifts between upper and lower anatomy paths for tighter escapes and bonus routes.
    • Risk Boost: Pass through a flashing organ gate -> You refresh your combo multiplier, but hazard speed and density spike for a few seconds.

    Plan

    1. Start in the torso of a giant grey anatomy chart with three connected lanes.
    2. Hazards stream inward in waves: scalpels cut across lanes, syringes thrust straight down paths, and anatomy labels slide in as blockers.
    3. Every few seconds one organ gate flashes, such as heart, lung, or brain.

    Pilot tips

    • Take organ gates early in calm waves; taking one during a dense wave is how big scores are made.
    • Scalpels usually sweep sideways, so save vertical dives for syringes and stacked blockers.
    • If your combo timer is low, a risky organ gate is usually worth more than playing safe.
