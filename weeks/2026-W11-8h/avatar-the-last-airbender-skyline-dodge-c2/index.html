    Mission briefing

    Ready to launch?

    Survive a 2-4 minute gauntlet of escalating elemental waves, using the last element you grazed to convert danger into combo points before three hits end the run.

    Controls

    • Dodge lanes: Swipe left or right -> The Avatar snaps between three sky lanes to avoid incoming hazards.
    • Ride the wind: Swipe up or down -> The Avatar moves between high and low altitude, slipping over rock spikes or under flame arcs.
    • Commit to a form: Brush the edge of a telegraphed elemental hazard -> That element becomes your current Avatar form for a few seconds, enabling score-charging passes through matching hazards

    Plan

    1. Elemental hazards stream in from the horizon: flame arcs, stone pillars, ice lances, and slicing gusts.
    2. You survive by swiping between lanes and altitudes while the pace ramps up every 20 seconds.
    3. The twist: the last element you safely graze becomes your current form, announced by a distinct musical sting and color shift.

    Pilot tips

    • Play safe between wave callouts; force a form change only when the next pattern is readable.
    • Use the audio cues: each element has its own warning sound, and the form-change sting confirms your risk paid off.
    • High scores come from chaining matched passes through dense waves, not from constant grazing.
