    Mission briefing

    Ready to launch?

    Survive a 2-4 minute run through escalating hazard waves, cashing in risky Rethink flips to post the highest leaderboard score before your three Hearts are gone.

    Controls

    • Dodge lanes: Swipe left or right -> Your spark snaps into a neighboring lane to avoid incoming blocks, walls, and shockwaves.
    • Hold the center line: No input -> You stay steady, useful for threading narrow gaps and setting up multiplier routes.
    • Trigger risk window: Swipe into a glowing Rethink Gate -> For a few seconds the rules invert: marked hazards become bonus targets to graze, while empty lanes become dangerous.

    Plan

    1. Start with three Hearts, a x1 multiplier, and slow descending waves of 'stale idea' blocks.
    2. Swipe between three to five vertical lanes as hazards fall faster and patterns get tighter every 20 seconds.
    3. Graze near hazards to build the Indie meter; close calls are worth more but a direct hit costs one Heart.

    Pilot tips

    • Build Indie meter with controlled near-misses instead of playing fully safe.
    • Do not auto-take every gate; a bad entry angle can turn the flip into a trap.
    • Use Rethink Mode to harvest marked hazards, then reposition before normal rules return.
