    Controls

    • Shift receiver: Swipe left or right -> Move the dish between skyline lanes to dodge incoming static, birds, and power surges.
    • Hold the lock: Keep your finger down while inside the glowing signal lane -> Decode the drifting transmission, build combo, and charge the score multiplier.
    • Bank the streak: Lift your finger before a Drift Flip hits -> Cash out the current multiplier safely instead of risking a full combo break.

    Plan

    1. Start on a rooftop with four lanes and a faint alien broadcast drifting between them.
    2. Swipe to dodge incoming static bursts and skyline hazards streaming downward in faster waves.
    3. When the glowing signal lane passes under your dish, stay inside it to decode packets and raise your multiplier.

    Pilot tips

    • Watch the audio pitch: the signal hum rises as the lane is about to drift.
    • Wrong-color pulses become dangerous only after each Drift Flip, so read the sky before committing.
    • Short swipes are safer early; long greedy locks win leaderboard runs.
