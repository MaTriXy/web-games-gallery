    Mission briefing

    Ready to launch?

    Survive a 3-minute official launch countdown in the Crimson Desert by dodging debris, flares, and collapsing scaffold pieces while choosing when to enter flashing clearance lanes that boost score but increase danger.

    Controls

    • Dodge lanes: Swipe left or right -> The skiff snaps between runway lanes to avoid hazards.
    • Hold the clearance beam: Keep your finger down while staying in the marked lane -> Builds the Official multiplier as long as you remain inside the beam without getting hit.
    • Break away: Swipe out of the marked lane at any time -> Banks the current multiplier before the beam expires or turns risky.

    Plan

    1. Start on a three-lane desert launch runway with a 180-second countdown.
    2. Debris streams in from the top: fuel drums, banner poles, flare rockets, and falling launch parts.
    3. Every few seconds, HQ marks one lane with an Official Clearance beam.

    Pilot tips

    • Do not sit in a clearance beam too long; the safest beam is rarely the highest-value beam.
    • Bank multipliers before mixed cross-lane waves, especially when flare sirens sound.
    • Audio helps: a clean chime means safe beam start, a harsher alarm means that lane is about to be punished.
