    Controls

    • Switch lanes: Swipe left or right -> Move the active loot box courier between the three conveyor lanes.
    • Seal a box: Tap a loot box -> Flip that crate shut so it can enter a quarantine gate instead of a color gate.
    • Cash out combo: Tap and hold for a brief beat on a glowing multiplier badge -> Bank the current combo multiplier before the next crackdown wave can break it.

    Plan

    1. Colored loot boxes roll toward matching gates on three lanes; send each open box through a gate of the same color.

    Pilot tips

    • Watch the warning siren: the next restricted color flashes a beat early.
    • Do not over-seal boxes; closed crates cannot score full color-match points.
    • Bank high multipliers before late-game crackdown waves stack two fast rule changes together.
