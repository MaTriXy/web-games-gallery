    Mission briefing

    Ready to launch?

    Survive a 3-minute launch route by switching lanes and matching your convoy color to incoming official gates while chaining perfect clears for a rising launch bonus.

    Controls

    • Switch lanes: Swipe left or right -> Moves the convoy to an adjacent lane.
    • Swap convoy color: Tap -> Cycles the convoy between red, gold, and blue clearance colors.
    • Arm launch mode: Press and hold during a countdown siren -> Locks your current color for 2 seconds, doubles gate value, and turns the next gate into an all-or-nothing launch check.

    Plan

    1. Start in the center lane with a convoy carrying an official launch core.
    2. Colored gates rush down three lanes; you must be in the matching lane and color when the convoy reaches a gate.
    3. Each clean gate adds combo and slightly increases speed.

    Pilot tips

    • Swipe early for lane changes; tap late for color changes.
    • Use launch mode only when the next gate pattern is readable.
    • Build combo on easy stretches, then cash in during countdown windows.
