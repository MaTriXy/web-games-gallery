    Mission briefing

    Ready to launch?

    Survive a 3-minute hiring spiral by dodging incoming hazards, collecting self-hire stamps, and cashing in short contract windows without getting buried by the business you created.

    Controls

    • Dodge lanes: Swipe left or right -> Move your avatar between rooftop lanes to avoid incoming hazards.
    • Fast dip: Swipe down -> Duck under flying paperwork and low drones for a brief moment.
    • Take the contract: Swipe up through a glowing HIRE gate -> Enter a short risk/reward phase that boosts score multiplier if you keep dodging cleanly.

    Plan

    1. Start as both the boss and the only employee on a three-lane rooftop office skyline.
    2. Dodge incoming hazards such as payroll invoices, HR paperwork stacks, recruiter drones, and burnout clouds.
    3. Collect floating self-hire stamps to build your combo meter.

    Pilot tips

    • Take HIRE gates only when the screen is crowded; the multiplier pays off most during dense waves.
    • Down-swipes are safer than panic lane changes when paperwork flies low.
    • Missing a stamp is fine; losing a hit point to force a combo is usually not.
