    Mission briefing

    Ready to launch?

    Survive a 2-4 minute after-hours rooftop escape while weaving through escalating office hazards, grabbing clock-out stamps to build a score multiplier, and avoiding three hits that end the run.

    Controls

    • Shift lanes: Swipe left or right -> Move between rooftop lanes to dodge incoming hazards and line up score pickups.
    • Burst step: Swipe up -> Hop over low hazards like cables, carts, and spilled coffee streams.
    • Duck slide: Swipe down -> Slip under high hazards like swinging signs, drones, and flying folders.

    Plan

    1. The player starts on a dusk skyline catwalk outside a glowing office tower, trying to make it home after work.
    2. Hazards stream in waves: paperwork gusts, rolling office chairs, dangling cables, delivery drones, and snapping neon signs.
    3. Every few seconds a 'clock-out stamp' spawns in the most dangerous lane; collecting it refreshes and increases the score multiplier.

    Pilot tips

    • Play for near-misses early; the base waves are slow enough to farm combo safely.
    • Do not chase every clock-out stamp if a launch shift siren just sounded.
    • Use burst step for low clutter only; lane movement is safer when hazards are stacked.
