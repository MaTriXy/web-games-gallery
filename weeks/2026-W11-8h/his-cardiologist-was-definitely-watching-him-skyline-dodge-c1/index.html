    Controls

    • Change lane: Swipe left or right -> Move the patient across three treadmill lanes to avoid incoming hazards.
    • Commit to risk: Swipe into a flashing red lane during a Definitely scan -> Charge the score multiplier faster, but spawn speed and hazard density increase until the scan ends.
    • Stabilize: Hold your lane during a green monitor pulse -> Bank the current multiplier and recover a small amount of heart meter if you stayed clean through the pulse.

    Plan

    1. Start on a monitored treadmill with a low heartbeat and a x1 multiplier.
    2. Dodge incoming hazards such as falling energy drinks, flying stair steps, panic alerts, and rolling office chairs.
    3. Every few seconds, the cardiologist triggers a big "DEFINITELY?" scan that marks one lane green and one lane red.

    Pilot tips

    • Use short swipes; overcorrecting into the red lane is the main way runs end.
    • Treat each Definitely scan as a decision point: cash out safely or push for one more boosted wave.
    • Audio matters: the faster heartbeat loop warns that risky streak speed is peaking.
