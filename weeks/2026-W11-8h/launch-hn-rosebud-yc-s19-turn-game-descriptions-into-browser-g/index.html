    Mission briefing

    Ready to launch?

    Keep the rosebud alive through a 2-4 minute launch sequence, survive escalating hazard waves, and cash in bloom multipliers for a local offline leaderboard score.

    Controls

    • Dodge lane: Swipe left or right -> The rosebud capsule snaps between three launch lanes to avoid incoming hazards.
    • Bloom burst: Tap and hold -> The rosebud opens for a moment, vacuuming nearby spark fragments for combo score but enlarging its hitbox.
    • Cash combo: Release hold before impact -> Bank the current bloom chain into a score multiplier; holding too long risks a crash.

    Plan

    1. Start in the center lane of a vertical launch shaft with slow-moving debris drifting downward.
    2. Swipe between lanes to dodge broken gantries, hot bolts, and collapsing browser-panel doors.
    3. Every few seconds a launch siren sounds and the shaft enters a 'Bloom Window': spark fragments appear between hazards.

    Pilot tips

    • Open the rosebud only when two spark lines overlap a safe lane.
    • Short bloom holds are safer early; long greedy blooms matter later when waves speed up.
    • Use audio: a rising chime means a Bloom Window is live, and a harsh buzzer warns the next hazard burst is entering.
