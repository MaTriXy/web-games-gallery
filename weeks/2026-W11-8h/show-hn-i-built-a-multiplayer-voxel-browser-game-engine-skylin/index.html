    Mission briefing

    Ready to launch?

    Survive 2-4 minute waves of incoming voxel hazards while chasing shifting host lanes to grow your score multiplier before the browser-city desyncs and knocks you out.

    Controls

    • Change lane: Swipe left or right -> Dash to the next rooftop lane to avoid incoming voxel traffic.
    • Vault: Swipe up -> Hop over low broken chunks and floor-level packet floods.
    • Sync the host: Press and hold on the glowing crown lane -> Charge multiplier and bank bonus score, but that lane spawns extra targeted hazards until the host swap ends.

    Plan

    1. Start on a three-lane voxel skyline with small debris streams and a steady chiptune beat.
    2. Swipe between lanes and vault over low hazards as bigger voxel buses, lag walls, and dropped player crates enter faster each wave.
    3. Every few seconds the glowing host crown jumps to a new lane; move there and hold to sync the multiplayer world for multiplier charge, or

    Pilot tips

    • Follow the warning beep: a low tone signals low obstacles you should vault, while a sharp chirp signals a side-lane impact.
    • Host swaps are the main score engine; skipping one is safer, but repeated skips let your multiplier drain away.
    • Use short holds on the crown lane when traffic is dense instead of committing to a full sync every time.
