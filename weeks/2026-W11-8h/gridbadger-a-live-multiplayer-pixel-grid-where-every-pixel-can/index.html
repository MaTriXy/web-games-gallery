    Mission briefing

    Ready to launch?

    Survive a 2-4 minute skyline run by dodging incoming cursor claims, escaping live tile eruptions, and building a score multiplier by crossing hot tiles just before they flip.

    Controls

    • Dash one tile: Swipe up, down, left, or right -> The gridbadger hops to the next rooftop tile in that direction.
    • Hold your nerve: Stay on a flashing tile for a moment -> You bait a takeover and earn a higher multiplier if you leave before the tile goes live.
    • Cut through surge lanes: Swipe through highlighted hot tiles during a takeover wave -> You bank bonus points fast, but a mistimed move gets you caught in the flip.

    Plan

    1. Start on a small rooftop grid while ghostly live cursors sweep in from the edges and mark tiles they are about to claim.
    2. Each claimed tile flips from safe to flashing to live-electric; standing on a tile when it turns live is an instant fail.
    3. Cursor paths get denser every wave, creating moving walls, cross-cuts, and corner traps that force quick lane changes.

    Pilot tips

    • Watch the cursors, not just the hazards; their approach tells you which escape lane will vanish next.
    • Use the center lanes early, then cut outward when surge tiles appear so you keep multiple exits open.
    • Don't chase every Price Surge; skipping a bad one is better than losing a full combo.
