    Ready to launch?

    Controls

    • Dodge: Swipe left or right -> The gridbadger hops to the next rooftop lane to avoid incoming cursors and closing tiles.
    • Cut Through: Swipe up through a glowing hot lane -> You cross a rising-price takeover lane for bonus points and combo gain if you clear it before it locks.
    • Bank Combo: Hold still on a safe dim tile for a brief beat -> Your current live multiplier converts into secured score, but you stop earning peak-risk bonuses.

    Plan

    1. Start on the center rooftop tile of a scrolling skyline grid.
    2. Rival live cursors streak in and begin flipping tiles from safe to claimed.
    3. Swipe lane to lane to stay on open tiles as takeover waves speed up.

    Pilot tips

    • Watch the ghost cursors, not just the tiles; their path shows which lanes will become unsafe next.
    • Hot lanes are worth the most right before lock, but waiting too long can leave no escape route.
    • Use each Live Flip pulse to reposition aggressively, because one lane always changes from bad to safe.
