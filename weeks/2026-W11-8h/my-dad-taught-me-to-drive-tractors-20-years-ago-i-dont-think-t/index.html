    Mission briefing

    Ready to launch?

    Survive a 2-4 minute driving lesson by entering the correct colored gates, obeying changing lesson calls, and building the highest combo before you run out of warnings.

    Controls

    • Switch lanes: Swipe left or right -> The tractor hops into the next crop lane.
    • Drift: Touch and hold while moving across lanes -> The tractor slides sideways and repaints itself to the lane color it enters.
    • Drive straight: Release before a gate -> The tractor enters cleanly and satisfies straight-driving lesson calls.

    Plan

    1. Start in one of three dirt lanes with a tractor color already active.
    2. Colored gates roll down the field; pass through a gate only if your tractor color matches it.
    3. Repaint by drifting into a new lane before the next gate reaches you.

    Pilot tips

    • Drift early when you need both a lane change and a repaint.
    • When Dad switches lessons, stop chasing the nearest gate and plan two gates ahead.
    • Use straight entries to protect a long combo; use drift entries to rescue bad color setups.
