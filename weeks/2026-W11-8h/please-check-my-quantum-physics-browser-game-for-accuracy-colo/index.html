    Mission briefing

    Ready to launch?

    Controls

    • Switch lane: Swipe left or right -> Move the review cursor to another colored lane before the next gate arrives.
    • Check a gate: Tap an incoming gate marked with a ? -> Reveal its true color for a moment so you can decide whether to stay, swap, or prepare a combo save.
    • Please slow: Press and hold the PLEASE button when charged -> Briefly slows the stream and shows the next two gates, but drains the charge immediately.

    Plan

    1. Start in the middle lane with a glowing review cursor.
    2. Colored gates stream downward; passing through a matching gate scores points and adds combo.
    3. A mismatched gate breaks combo and costs one heart.

    Pilot tips

    • Save PLEASE for entangled waves, not single uncertain gates.
    • Tap ? gates early; the reveal window is short.
    • A high combo matters more than chasing every risky bonus check.
