    Controls

    • Switch lanes: Swipe left or right -> Move your token to a safer or more profitable lane before the next gate lands
    • Swap color: Tap -> Flip your token between the two active colors to match the next gate
    • Take a risk choice: Stay in the lane marked Worked or Didn't when the split arrives -> Worked boosts multiplier and speed; Didn't banks combo cash and soft-resets the pattern

    Plan

    1. Start each run at $0 with three receipt hearts and a x1 multiplier.
    2. Match your token color to incoming gates to earn cash; consecutive matches raise the combo multiplier.
    3. Every few seconds, a split wave stamps one lane as WORKED and another as DIDN'T.

    Pilot tips

    • Use DIDN'T when your color lock would trap you in a bad lane pattern.
    • Chain short WORKED streaks early, then bank before the speed spike becomes unmanageable.
