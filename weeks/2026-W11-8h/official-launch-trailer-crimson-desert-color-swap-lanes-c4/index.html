    Mission briefing

    Ready to launch?

    Survive a 2-4 minute desert run by switching between three lanes, matching your sled color to incoming official gate seals, and using the trailer-launch twist to vault blocked patterns for the highest leaderboard score.

    Controls

    • Change lanes: Swipe left or right -> Moves the launch sled into an adjacent lane.
    • Swap permit color: Tap the sled -> Cycles the sled between red, blue, and gold to match the next gate.
    • Launch trailer: Press and hold, then release -> Fires the attached trailer forward to break one gate row or collect a distant combo line, but leaves you without that

    Plan

    1. Start in the center lane with a color-coded official permit on your sled.
    2. Incoming gate rows roll down the desert in three lanes; pass only through a gate that matches your current permit color.
    3. Wrong-color contact or hitting a sealed barrier costs your trailer if attached; the next mistake ends the run.

    Pilot tips

    • Read the next two rows before swapping color; late lane changes are safer than panic color cycling.
    • Save trailer launches for double-blocked patterns or priority gates you cannot line up normally.
    • If an Official Order changes the hot color, keep combo alive first and chase bonus second.
