Objective

Clear marked relay orbs before their countdown rings empty, keep your squad link alive for 2-4 minutes, and build a combo multiplier by chaining accurate taps.

Controls

  • Pop relay orb: Tap a glowing orb -> Destroys the orb, adds score, and extends your combo if hit before it vanishes.
  • Prioritize danger lane: Tap orbs with red lock rings first -> Prepares incoming missile lanes and prevents squad shield loss.
  • Recover pressure: Tap a blue sync orb -> Briefly slows spawn speed and stabilizes the arena.

How to Play

  1. Neon relay orbs streak across 3 clear air lanes toward your multiplayer squad beacon.
  2. Each orb shows a shrinking ring and lane arrow so you can read exactly which target will expire first.
  3. Tap relay orbs before they vanish; missed red-lock orbs trigger missile hits on the squad shield.
  4. Successful taps in a row raise a combo multiplier, while misses or late taps break it.
  5. Every 30 seconds, orb speed increases, mixed lane patterns appear, and decoy gray orbs start crossing the field.
  6. The run ends when the squad shield takes 3 missile hits or the timer expires; your score posts to a simple local leaderboard.

Tips

  • Follow the shrinking rings, not just the motion; they telegraph urgency.
  • Red-lock orbs are fail-state threats and should override normal targets.
  • Blue sync orbs are rare recovery windows when the screen gets crowded.
  • Fast, accurate single taps beat frantic double tapping because misses break combo.
