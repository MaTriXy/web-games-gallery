    Mission briefing

    Ready to launch?

    Defend your tiny multiplayer air squad by tapping missile pings on-beat, chaining perfect interceptions, and keeping the formation alive until the timer ends.

    Controls

    • Intercept missile: Tap a falling missile ring as it closes over your squad marker -> Destroys the missile and adds to combo if timed on-beat
    • Swap lane: Tap and hold, then slide left or right across the three sky lanes -> Moves your squad marker to the busiest lane for the next beat
    • Burst cancel: Two-finger tap when the meter is full -> Clears all current missiles on screen and preserves combo through a dense wave

    Plan

    1. Missiles descend in three air lanes toward your multiplayer squad formation at the center of the screen.
    2. Each missile has a shrinking beat ring; tap exactly as the ring meets the squad marker to score a Perfect, Good, or Miss.
    3. Perfect taps build combo and raise a score multiplier; missed beats break combo and remove one shield plane from the formation.

    Pilot tips

    • Watch the shrinking ring, not the missile sprite.
    • Slide lanes early during quieter bars so you are set for dense choruses.
    • Save Burst Cancel for mixed real-missile and decoy patterns.
