    Mission briefing

    Ready to launch?

    Survive a 3-minute airmash by tapping missile lock rings in rhythm before they hit your squad, building combo multipliers and posting the highest score on the leaderboard.

    Controls

    • Deflect lock: Tap a lane ring as its lock reticle closes on the beat -> Missile is jammed, beat continues, combo increases
    • Emergency dodge: Swipe left or right when a red warning wave flashes -> Your squad shifts lanes and avoids an unblockable volley
    • Charge burst: Hold on a glowing center beacon through one full beat -> Releases a pulse that clears all current locks for bonus points

    Plan

    1. Missile locks spawn in 3 lanes around your squadron, each synced to the music pulse.
    2. Tap each lock exactly on the beat to jam it before impact.
    3. Every 10 clean hits raises your score multiplier by 1x until you miss.

    Pilot tips

    • Watch the shrinking lock ring, not just the missile sprite.
    • Save the burst hold for crowded double-lock sections.
    • Swipe early on red waves; they cannot be tapped away.

    45-second run. No page scrolling. Beat your best combo.
