Weekly concept 2: hyper-casual reflex challenge

Score 0 Combo 0 Multiplier x1 Best 0 Time 45

Objective

Survive a 3-minute delivery run by dodging incoming rooftop hazards, grabbing glowing concept notes, and building the highest score before the paper stack is destroyed.

Controls

  • Dodge lanes: Swipe left or right -> The editor cart snaps to the next rooftop lane to avoid hazards.
  • Cut through gaps: Swipe up -> A short burst moves the cart forward through tight hazard patterns.
  • Cash in risk: Tap with two fingers -> Bank the current combo into a temporary score shield, but reset the multiplier.

How to Play

  1. Start with three paper hearts and a x1 multiplier.
  2. Hazards stream down the rooftop lanes: drones, billboards, loose vents, and red-ink storms.
  3. Swipe between lanes to survive while collecting floating concept notes placed close to danger lines.
  4. Every note collected without taking damage increases the multiplier; grazing hazards fills a risk meter faster.
  5. Waves speed up every 30 seconds, mix patterns more aggressively, and end when all hearts are lost.

Tips

  • Stay near the center lane so late wave patterns leave you two escape routes.
  • Grab concept notes only when they extend an escape path, not when they trap you against a wall.
  • Bank a high multiplier right before dense waves if survival matters more than greed.
  • Use the upward burst sparingly; it saves runs, but mistiming it can place you inside the next pattern.
