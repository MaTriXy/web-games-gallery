Weekly Concept: Pulse Brief

Memorize the weekly concept board's flashing pulse order, then replay it before the deadline siren hits.

Weekly concept 1: brain teaser with playful pressure

Score 0 Combo 0 Multiplier x1 Best 0 Time 45

Objective

Tap the concept tiles in the exact pulse sequence to ship as many weekly briefs as possible before you run out of strikes.

Controls

  • Replay sequence: Tap concept tiles in order -> Correct taps lock in the brief and build combo
  • Hold to preview: Press and hold the preview button -> Spend a limited assist to hear and see the current sequence once more
  • Start next brief: Tap the ready stamp -> Begins the next faster, longer pulse round

How to Play

  1. A new weekly board appears with 4 concept tiles such as Hook, Color, Shape, and Mood.
  2. The tiles pulse with light and distinct audio tones in a short sequence.
  3. After the preview ends, replay the exact order by tapping the tiles.
  4. Each cleared brief adds one pulse to future rounds and slightly shortens preview time and input time.
  5. A wrong tap or expired timer costs a strike; after 3 strikes, the session ends and your leaderboard score is submitted locally for later syn
  6. Every few rounds, a bonus brief appears with a faster beat and doubled combo value.

Tips

  • Listen as much as you look; each tile has its own tone.
  • Short, fast taps are safer than hesitating near the timer.
  • Use the preview assist only on long sequences, because assists are limited.
  • Bonus briefs are the best way to push leaderboard scores if your combo is already high.
